Up coming events / Takahanga

Getting the kids involved / Whakauru tamariki

Join us as we transform our surroundings into a vibrant garden haven, buzzing with laughter, exploration, and the sweet fragrance of flowers. Through hands-on workshops, engaging activities, and interactive demonstrations, children will unlock the secrets of the soil, discover the wonders of plant life, and learn how their actions can positively impact the world around them.

Climate protest / Mautohe

n this pivotal moment, we recognize that climate change transcends borders, ideologies, and generations. It affects every corner of the globe, disproportionately impacting the most vulnerable communities. Today, we amplify their voices, advocating for climate justice and standing in solidarity with those on the frontlines of this crisis. Through peaceful protest, passionate speeches, and creative expressions, we aim to raise awareness, spark conversations, and inspire concrete actions. We demand bold commitments to reduce greenhouse gas emissions, transition to renewable energy, and protect our invaluable ecosystems.

Recycle for schools / Hangarua

At our school, we believe in the power of collective action and the impact it can have on our surroundings. "Clean and Green" is not just a cleanup event; it's an opportunity for students, teachers, and staff to come together and create a positive change in our immediate community.
